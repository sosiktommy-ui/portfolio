Project 03

AI Voice Auto-Responder & Booking Assistant

A voice-first AI assistant for inbound calls, booking requests, and operational answers. I worked on a system built around Twilio voice webhooks, Groq/OpenAI-compatible AI flows, speech handling, reservation capture, SMS confirmations, and SQLite logging, with support tooling for monitoring, health checks, and safe fallback behavior.

  • Handles inbound calls as a product flow, not just a single endpoint.
  • Uses a parser and cached information layer so answers can be grounded in current venue data.
  • Combines voice automation, business rules, local persistence, and operator-minded fallbacks.
AI voice flows Twilio webhooks Site parsing Booking automation SQLite logs SMS confirmation

Voice orchestration

Inbound call -> recognition -> intent -> reservation capture -> confirmation -> logs.

The interesting part here is not only the AI layer. It is the orchestration between phone entrypoints, dialogue state, live business information, booking capture, health checks, and safe fallback behavior.

Voice automation

Webhook-based call intake, structured dialogue handling, short AI answers, and operator-ready routing.

Live information layer

Website parsing with cache so the assistant can answer using current events, address, timings, prices, and venue context.

Business logic

Reservation capture for date, time, guest count, caller number, and booking history with local persistence.

Operational reliability

Health endpoints, logs, tunnel-ready setup, and safe fallback responses for real call traffic.
