Project 03

AI Voice Auto-Responder and Booking Assistant

A voice-first AI assistant for inbound calls, booking requests, and operational answers. Built around Twilio webhooks, Groq/OpenAI flows, speech handling, and SQLite logging.

  • Handles inbound calls as a product flow, not just a single endpoint.
  • Uses a parser and cached layer so answers are grounded in current venue data.
  • Combines voice automation, business rules, and operator-minded fallbacks.
